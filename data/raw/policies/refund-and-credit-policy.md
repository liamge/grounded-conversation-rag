# Refund and Credit Policy
Doc ID: policy-refund-credit
Effective: 2025-07-15

Service credits
- Issued only for verified platform downtime or invoice errors.
- Credit equals prorated downtime beyond SLA: monthly_fee * (minutes_downtime / 43,200).
- Minimum credit: 5% of monthly fee when SLA breach is confirmed.

Partial refunds
- Allowed when customer never used the billed add-on. Refund prorated to unused days.
- If usage is present but below 5%, offer a 10% goodwill credit instead of refund.

Process
1) Verify incident ID or billing evidence.
2) Confirm account owner approval.
3) Apply credit in billing system and email confirmation within 1 business day.
