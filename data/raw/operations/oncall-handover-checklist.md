# On-Call Handover Checklist
Doc ID: ops-oncall-handover
Version: 2025-09-12

- Review open incidents and confirm owners; unresolved P1/P2 must not be handed off without a live call.
- Post a shift summary in #oncall-handoff with: incidents, mitigations in place, follow-ups, and customer promises.
- Update the Ops Journal in Notion with links to logs, dashboards, and any temporary flags.
- Ensure alerts are snoozed or transferred; keep PagerDuty override only if successor acknowledges.
- Verify runbooks used and gaps discovered; open tasks in Jira under the "Reliability" project.
